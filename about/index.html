My responsibilities in the Coast Guard of Chile focused on the protection of lives at sea, as well as being a guardian of the marine environment and natural resources. My roles included:

  • Commander of Patrol Boat: Overseeing the complete operation of a rescue patrol boat in the Patagonian fjords of southern Chile. Leading a crew of 8 in various maritime activities, such as search and rescue, lighthouse maintenance and supporting local communities.
